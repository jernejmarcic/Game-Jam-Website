Welcome to Our Game Jam

We are hosting a game jam as part of our CAS project.

We invite everyone to submit any games they made on this website to be tested and ranked by us. The competition will last to the June 20th of 2023 and anyone with an authentic game can join.

Theme we have decided on is:

JUNGLE

Any games which have implemented this will receive bonus points, but don’t worry if your game isn’t related to the theme as you still have good chances. The winner of this competition will also get a reward so be sure to participate!

Rules

  • Theme: All games must focus on animals and feature challenges that require animals to use their natural traits to solve them (the traits can be exagaretd obviously, as an example you can use a kanguru character to pass a parkour section).
  • Genres: We welcome all types of games - from text-based adventures and simple 2D platformers, to intricate strategy games, immersive RPGs, or even fully-realized, ray-traced AAA games (like anyone would actually do that).
  • Originality: All assets (including but not limited to art, sound, and code) used in the game must be your own, or used with permission/under an appropriate license. Plagiarism may not be tolerated, depedning how we feel.
  • Development Window: Games must be developed entirely within the given time frame of the jam. Early planning, such as creating game concepts and ideas, is allowed, so is submitting old games, it's not like we'll check.
  • Submission: All games must be submitted by the deadline (June 20 2023, midnight). Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • Team Size: You can work alone or in a team. There are no limitations on team size, but we encourage solo work.
  • Language: While we welcome all entries, please note that the game interface and all accompanying documentation/submissions should be in English for judging purposes.
  • Rating: Games don't have to be suitable for all ages. Any content that is deemed to be offensive or overly violent will get extra points.
